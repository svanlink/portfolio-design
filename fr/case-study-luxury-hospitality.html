The Brief

A five-star property on the shores of Lake Geneva needed a brand film that would speak to ultra-high-net-worth travelers — not with flashy visuals, but with restraint. The brief emphasized silence, texture, and a sense of timelessness.

The film would live on the hotel's website, private booking portals, and be shown to guests on arrival via in-room screens. Every frame had to feel like an invitation, not a sales pitch.

02

The Approach

We shot at dawn and dusk — the hours when the property feels most alive. No talent. No narration. Just the architecture breathing: light moving across marble, steam rising from the spa, linen catching wind on a terrace overlooking the lake.

The movement was glacially slow — long tracking shots on a slider, subtle dolly moves through corridors. The sound design was built around ambient textures: water, birds, the click of a door latch. The goal was ASMR-level intimacy.
