01

The Brief

A Swiss-based tech startup was preparing to launch their first consumer product — a wearable designed for outdoor athletes. They needed a launch film that could anchor their crowdfunding campaign and set the tone for the brand going forward.

The challenge: make a product that doesn't exist yet feel essential. The film had to communicate both the technology and the emotion behind it — why someone would care enough to back it.

02

The Approach

We split the film into two acts. Act one: the problem — a runner alone in the mountains, disconnected, navigating by instinct. Act two: the product — the same runner, same mountain, but now with data, confidence, and clarity. The transition happens in a single, unbroken shot.

Product shots were captured in a controlled tabletop studio setup with macro lenses — close enough to see the texture of the band, the glow of the display. These were intercut with the outdoor narrative to create a rhythm between intimacy and scale.
