The Brief

Adidas Terrex needed a campaign film that would redefine how trail running is perceived — not just as sport, but as a meditative encounter with nature. The brief was clear: capture the raw intensity of mountain running in the Swiss Alps while keeping the athlete's emotional journey at the center.

The film had to work across digital, social, and retail screens — meaning it needed to feel cinematic at full scale but hold attention in a 9:16 scroll.

02

The Approach

We shot over four days at altitude, chasing golden hour across three different mountain passes. The creative direction leaned into contrast — the stillness of dawn against the violence of a sprint, silence broken only by breathing and gravel.

The camera work combined stabilized tracking shots with handheld close-ups to create an intimate but expansive feel. We shot on the RED Komodo with Cooke anamorphics to give the landscape an epic, widescreen texture.
