The Brief

Decathlon Switzerland was repositioning its brand in the Swiss market — moving away from "budget sports retailer" toward a brand that genuinely democratizes sport. They needed a film that captured real people, real moments, and the quiet joy of moving your body.

The challenge: make an accessible brand feel aspirational without losing its warmth. No professional athletes. No perfect bodies. Just honest, beautiful movement.

02

The Approach

We cast real Decathlon customers and employees — a trail runner who started at 45, a father teaching his daughter to cycle, a group of friends discovering bouldering. The film weaves their stories into a single thread about the universal feeling of starting something new.

Visually, we leaned into natural light and handheld work to keep it grounded. The grade was warm and desaturated — Swiss autumn tones with just enough contrast to feel cinematic without feeling staged.
